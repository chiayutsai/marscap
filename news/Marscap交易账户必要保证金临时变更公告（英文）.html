Temporary Margin changes to your Marscap Trading Account


Due to increased market volatility as the number of confirmed COVID-19 cases rises globally, the margin requirements for Marscap Margin FX and CFD products will change temporarily.


Effective immediately:

The margin requirement for UK 100 Index Futures CFD (FT) , SGX Japan 225 Index Futures CFD (NK), and HongKong50 Index Futures CFD (HS) will increase from 2% to 5% effective immediately.

Taking effect on 13 March 2020 when the market opens:

1) For ALL FX products the margin requirement will increase from 1% to 3%.
2) For ALL Commodity CFD products and Indices CFD products (except for FT, NK and HS) the margin requirement will increase from 2% to 5%.
Notes: Above margin requirements includes both the necessary margin and necessary margin for maintenance.

Please check the details above to determine what action (if any) you need to take in respect of your positions and trades. If you need to make a payment, please allow sufficient time for it to reach us. Where you do not have sufficient funds in your Account to meet your margin requirements, you may be subject to an Account Close-Out. We recommend that you monitor both your Account and any notice on our website . http://marscap.co.nz/ carefully so that you can keep track of any subsequent changes
